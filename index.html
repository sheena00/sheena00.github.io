1. Data Extraction & Processing
Data Collection | ETL | Cleaning & Analysis

I design data workflows from raw data collection to structured, analysis-ready datasets. This includes survey design, database querying, validation checks, cleaning (R,Stata, python), and statistical analysis to generate findings. Last month, I contributed to the analysis of youth climate-induced migration study. We examined why young people move, their living and work conditions after arriving in secondary cities, and how their livelihoods, agency, and wellbeing differ by gender and age. The analysis showed that slow-onset livelihood decline, such as declining crop yields and food insecurity, was the main driver of migration, female migrants tended to move at a younger age and were more likely to live with partners and in larger households, whereas male migrants were more independent. We also looked at livelihoods: women were more likely to be unemployed or in informal work, while men had more formal or higher-return jobs. Gender, age, and education were the strongest factors influencing work type, agency, and economic outcomes.

5. Systems Management & Security
Web Management | Incident Response | Security Controls

I oversee system performance and integrity, implement security controls and maintain web infrastructure to protect data assets. From December 2024 to May 2025, a website I managed was hacked through an outdated Astra theme, which created hundreds of hidden spam pages with Japanese keywords. The hack persisted even after initial fixes. I diagnosed the issue, identified a backdoor, a regenerating script and removed all compromised files. I strengthened the site’s security by limiting access, enabling two-factor authentication, disabling file editing, set up a monitoring plugin for real time alerts and adding firewall protection via Cloudflare. Over several months of monitoring, the website was fully cleaned, spam pages were removed from search engine indexing, and no further unauthorized access has occurred.
